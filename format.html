The Format

Testcases & Number of elements :

You will notice the required number of input boxes available and click submit.


The 4 elements per testcase :

1stly, choose int (for integer),
sp-int (for space separated integer values),
char (for a character), or
string (a string).


Your 2nd and 3rd elements are the lower bound and upper bound of the value required, whenever applicable.
In case of string, they signify min and max length of the string.


Your 4th element denotes the number of space-separated integers.


Example : 

                        int 1 1000
                        char
                        sp-int 1 100 5
                        string 4 40

will give testcases in the following form : 
                        823
                        f
                        34 13 83 92 8
                        kjsdnldide
